How to Get Started

1

Connect Your Battle.net Account

Securely link your Battle.net account using OAuth 2.0. We never store your password - only access tokens that let us fetch your character data safely.

2

Select Your Region

Choose your WoW region (US, EU, etc.) to ensure we're tracking the right characters. You can switch regions anytime from the dashboard.

3

Refresh Your Character Data

Click "Refresh Data" to pull all your level 10+ characters from Battle.net. This includes names, levels, specs, guilds, professions, and more.

4

Populate Quest Cache (Optional)

For quest tracking, click "Populate Quest Cache" to scan all your characters' completed quests. This builds a comprehensive database for zone completion tracking.

5

Explore and Optimize

Use the dashboard to filter characters, explore the character manager for detailed views, and check quest zones to find missing content!

The "Populate Quest Cache" process can take a few minutes for players with many characters and thousands of completed quests. It runs in the background, so you can continue using the app while it works!
